Discover Bürglen

The village at the entrance to the Schächental is perfect for a bike. Take a leisurely bike over green fields and through beautiful forests. On the Kapellenweg you can marvel at numerous chapels in Bürglen and discover the village.

The mythical mountain landscape inspired our ancestors to create mysterious legends. Here you can read the four most famous sagas - let yourself be captivated by the scary stories.


Altdorf

Altdorf is the economic, political, cultural and social center of the canton of Uri. You are here at around 450 m above sea level. in the lower Reuss valley, south of Lake Lucerne, surrounded by an impressive mountain panorama. The Italianità of the architecture, squares and alleys of Altdorf let you feel the proximity to the south. In addition to the wide range of cultural activities that Altdorf has to offer, you are here at the perfect starting point for numerous hikes and excursions into the mountains.


Schattdorf

Schattdorf is located in the Urner Reuss Valley south of Altdorf at 481 m above sea level and is known as the Landsgemeindedorf. From the beginning of the 15th century until May 1928, the citizens of Uri met here once a year to do political business. It is an idyllic village, which is surrounded by a lot of forest and so offers numerous opportunities to move in nature. Let off steam while doing sports - on the sports field, jogging in the forest or in the bouldering hall.


Flüelen

The village of Flüelen is a great starting point for your next travelling on bike. The Eggberge and Oberaxen areas are accessible by cable cars and are very popular. Discover the tradition of wild hay on the wild hay path on the Rophaien. Or embark on the Swiss Path, which begins on the Rütli and leads through Flüelen.


Museums & history
Numerous treasures from the history of Uri allow you to immerse yourself in the past of the country and people of Uri. Uri has an impressive number of museums in relation to the number of inhabitants.
